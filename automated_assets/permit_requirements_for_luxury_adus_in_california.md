## Step-by-Step Implementation Guide

### Pre-Permitting Phase (Weeks 1-4)

* **Review Local Zoning Regulations**: Ensure compliance with local zoning ordinances and regulations.
* **Conduct Site Analysis**: Assess the site's topography, environmental conditions, and neighboring properties.
* **Prepare Permit Applications**: Gather all necessary documents, including building plans, elevations, and cross-sections.

### Permit Submittal Phase (Weeks 5-8)

* **Submit Building Permit Application**: Include detailed plans, specifications, and calculations.
* **Submit Zoning Permit Application**: Provide supporting documentation, such as environmental impact assessments and zoning variance requests (if applicable).
* **Submit Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC, and Fire Permit Applications**: Ensure compliance with relevant electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and fire safety codes.

### Permit Processing Phase (Weeks 9-12)

* **Waiting Period**: Allow sufficient time for the reviewing authority to process permit applications.
* **Permit Inspection**: Schedule a site inspection to ensure compliance with permit requirements.

### Post-Permitting Phase (After Week 12)

* **Obtain Final Inspections**: Schedule and complete final inspections to ensure satisfactory completion of work.
* **Receive Certificate of Occupancy**: Receive the certificate, indicating that the ADU is ready for occupancy.
